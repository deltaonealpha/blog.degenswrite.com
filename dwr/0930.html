DEGENSWRITE CORPORATION

Reading Lists v3.0, "likepaper", continue from where you left off, and more!

Welcome to the next monthly incremental set of updates to degenswrite, the 0.9.3.0. Read on, for the full changelog.

Presenting "likepaper"

Title page floating toolbar now has an option under "themes" to make the page look like paper. This can be applied across themes.
Your preference is saved and synced across devices on your account.


Continue Where You Left-Off

A tile is shown on the home page's top section with the details of the literature you were reading the last. Tap the tile to go to your last reading location on that title.

Reading Lists v3.0

- You can now choose between your two primary reading lists for which one to add a title to.


- Now view both your primary reading lists with previews from the floating reading list window.

- Your 2nd primary reading list is now sharable too, with degenswrite shareURLs.
- Enabled adding and viewing the 2nd primary reading list.

Others

- Theme buttons are now narrower in width.
- Fixed "Nature Sounds" said "Share this title" on floating toolkit on title pages.
- Fixed pre-floating div padding on title pages.

Incremental Changes:
- Globally incremented release ID and build details.
- Added latest changelog link to all placeholders.
- Furnished latest changelog on blog.degenswrite.com
