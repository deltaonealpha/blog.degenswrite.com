Float! design, new support options with account access control permissions and more!

Welcome to the next weekly incremental set of updates to degenswrite, the 0.9.2.5. Read on, for the full changelog.

Introducing Float! Design

Float! Design, the next major update to degenswrite's design makes the navbar and the footer "float" on top of the existing background, giving the illusion of a three-dimensional effect.
Float! Design also brings about colour and border-radius changes to elements it is applied on.

Navbar:
- Navbar and footer now float on-top of the cover and footer image on every page, incl. title pages.
- Changed navbar colours from #1B212A >>> to >>> #0a1018c5, globally.
- Navbar now does not show self-links (example: community page's navbar doesn't show community link now).
- Every page's navbar now says "GOOD MORNING/ AFTERNOON/ EVENING", not only the home page incl. title pages.
- Navbar links are now smaller in size (14px >> 11px) and now show in #d1d1d1 instead of #ffffff.


degenswrite Search:
- Changed search bar colours from #1B212A >>> to >>> #0a101869, globally.

Changes to Title Pages

- Floating toolkit on title pages now shows the time in a minimal and understated way with an icon indicating "present" on the top; is left-aligned.

- Removed indicating remenants of option to disable alphaUI design since alphaUI is now enforced since update 0.8.x.x.

New Support Option - Support Key

- Added Settings option (method) "ENTER SUPPORT KEY". Enter a key given by degenswrite support to view a confirmatory page (you agree to giving full account access). "AGREE" to process support key instructions.

Others

- "Poems" and Home pages now also show tips.

- Renamed "dulcinea" to "float!"

- Discontinued "whatsnew.html" in favour of blog.degenswrite.com. All previous updates and their telegraph links have been migrated over.
Old "whatsnew" page now automatically redirects you to blog.degenswrite.com.

- Fixed issue where checking-in did not change the button text until a re-tap on the button (daily continuous streaks).

- Replaced "Latest Update" on Community page with a link to blog.degenswrite's top- most post.
